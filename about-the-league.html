About the league

The Columbus Vanguards are proudly competing in the largest league in women's tackle football in the world. The Women's Football Alliance has over 65 teams nationwide and continue to grow each season. Adding more competition while giving smaller market cities like the Columbus Vanguards a chance in the Big Leagues! 💪😎🏈

We are excited and honored to be a part of this league and the history it is making for the Women's Tackle Football World.
