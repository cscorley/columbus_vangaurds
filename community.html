To be a part of a community, you must first be involved in the community. -- Vanguards


The Columbus Vanguards are determined to make change on and off the field. As a team that does not require "PLAYER FEES", we commit our time volunteering around our cities. The Columbus Vanguards commit to 44 hours a year as a team. However, individuals/companies can earn community day hours by becoming a SPONSOR. Which will be completed by the team in the sponsor's name.

Columbus Vanguards had their first Community Day and boy was it huge! Filling up more than 9 Lowe's (donation) Buckets of trash in and around Mill Race Park, Columbus, IN. Thank you to all the Lady Vanguards that came out to accomplish this important task to keep parks and streets clean for Columbus and their youth.


The Columbus Vanguards are at it again, this time reaching out into the sister city of Franklin, IN. The Head Start program reached out to the Columbus Vanguards in desperate need of volunteers. The mission was to make the playground safer for the children, by mulching the entire area. It was a great pleasure to work along the Head Start program in this most important task.


Would you like to help volunteer with the Columbus Vanguards in Columbus and surrounding areas? Check out our Community Event Calendar, or contact us!

Do you have ideas and contacts of possible Vanguards Community Day events? Please ask us! All request for our help will be discussed with the board of directors and the team. Our criterion are:

  • Must be within a 30 min drive from Columbus, IN
  • Benefits the Community
  • Youth or Non-Profit organizations
  • Tuesdays or Thursdays during volunteer times (6pm-8pm)
  • Must be submitted 2-4 weeks prior for approval

If they fall on a different day or time, please still submit! We'd love to find a way to help.
